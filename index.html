About

In Ethiopia there are few e-commerce sites. All of them are based in the capital city Addis Abeba. All of these sites services are focused for Addis Abeba residents. So we want to solve this problem. We want to provide an online market for residents of other cities with simple and clean UI. Also with multiple product choices. Currently our main users are Ethiopia residents. As we grow we expand our product to East Africa as well as to Africa. This project is not a specific locale. It's a scalable project.
